POIJU.IO

Welcome to POIJU.IO developer documentation. POIJU.IO is free service for nautical charts for Finland sea and lake areas.

Want to use POIJU.IO?

Go to www.poiju.io! using a browser of your choice on laptop, desktop, mobile device or tablet. Remember to allow location services on your device.
